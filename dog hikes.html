Dog Hike

Dog Hike at our Pet resort


For the adventurous pups, Pet Recourir offers exhilarating dog hikes through scenic trails surrounding our resort. Led by experienced guides, our dog hikes provide the perfect opportunity for dogs to explore the great outdoors, burn off excess energy, and indulge their natural curiosity. Whether your dog is a seasoned trailblazer or a first-time hiker, our guided hikes offer a refreshing and invigorating experience for dogs of all ages and fitness levels.


What do you need to start?

  1. Your dog will need to be up-to-date on all of their vaccines and must have their rabies tag attached to them. Dogs without a rabies tag can be fined at the parks we frequent.
  2. Your dog will need to have basic leash walking skills and be comfortable walking in close proximity with other dogs.
  3. Each dog should have a form of tick prevention and be vaccinated for tick borne illnesses.
  4. Every dog must wear a properly fitted harness, preferably one with a front clip option such as an “Easy Walk” or a “Freedom No Pull Harness”. Leashes will be provided by Pet Recourir Pet Resort.
  5. Your dog should wear an ID tag with their name and contact information in case of an emergency.
  6. Your dog must be friendly with others. It’s important to us that each pup has a great time so that means our hikers should get along. We’re trained to notice any turn in play that could result in aggression to further ensure everyone’s safety.
