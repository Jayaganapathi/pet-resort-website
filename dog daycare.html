Dog daycare at our Pet resort


At Pet Recourir, we understand the importance of socialization and exercise for dogs. Our dog day care service provides a safe and stimulating environment for your furry companion to interact with other dogs and engage in fun activities under the supervision of our trained staff. Whether your dog loves to play fetch, enjoys splashing in pools, or simply wants to lounge in the sun, our spacious indoor and outdoor play areas offer plenty of opportunities for enjoyment and exercise.


Dog Daycare resort features

  1. Indoor flooring consists of slip-resistant, reduced impact rubber
  2. Outdoor areas utilize specialized K9 Grass for easy traction and sanitary conditions
  3. Daycare/exercise specialists provide constant supervision for safe play
  4. All play areas are cleaned and sterilized daily to provide a sanitary environment
  5. Pictures posted to our Facebook page daily
