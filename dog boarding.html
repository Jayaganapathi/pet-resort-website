Dog Boarding at our Pet resort


When you need to be away from home, you can trust Pet Recourir to provide a comfortable home away from home for your dog. Our dog boarding facilities are equipped with cozy accommodations and personalized attention to ensure your pet feels relaxed and pampered during their stay. From spacious suites to regular walks and playtime, we strive to make your dog's boarding experience as enjoyable as possible.


Dog Boarding resort features

  1. Indoor flooring consists of slip-resistant, reduced impact rubber
  2. Outdoor areas utilize specialized K9 Grass for easy traction and sanitary conditions
  3. Crate-free luxury suites allow your dog to feel comfortable and at home during their stay
  4. Daycare included in overnight stays
  5. 24/7 care and supervision from our professionally trained staff
  6. Specialized air filtration system to reduce contaminants and airborne illnesses
  7. All suites are cleaned and sterilized daily to provide a sanitary environment
  8. Continuous soothing music played to promote a stress-free, relaxing experience
  9. Hypoallergenic elevated beds included in each suite
  10. Beautifully designed suites provide the ultimate in overnight canine accommodations
